WindRunners

The WindRunners are one of ten orders of the Knights Radiant. Selfless service best describes WindRunner's core belief. WindRunners control the surges of Gravitation, the ability to manipulate gravity, and adhesion, the ability to bond items together. The WindRunner's ability to control the surges comes from the Nahel Bond created with Honor Spren.

Windrunner are found on the planet Roshar, in the City of Urithiru.

As with all Knights Radiant, the Windrunners swear the first ideal to access their surges.

    Immortal Words
  • Strength before Weakness. Life before Death. Journey before Destination
  • I will protect those who cannot protect themselves
  • I will protect those I hate so long as it is right
  • I accept that there will be those I cannot protect
