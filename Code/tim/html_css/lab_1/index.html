Teemo is a Yordle from League of Legends. He is regarded as a legend by fellow yordles. Teemo is friendly and genuine but can switch into a highly focused combative mindset and be a lethal weapon.
Teemo's home is in Bandle City and he really only leaves when it's time to go on a mission, which he does alone. He has prowess in prolonged missions in the:
  • Jungle
  • Desert
  • Arctic
