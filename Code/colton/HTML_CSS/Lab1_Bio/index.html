David Arora

During the rainy season, many mushroom lovers head to BLM land or national forests to collect wild mushrooms sprouting from the forest floor. Mycologist David Arora, author of "Mushrooms Demystified", "All That the Rain Promises, and More ...," and "Economic Botany - The Mushroom Edition" has moved from the city to a ridge outside Gualala, above the southern Mendocino County coast.
"To mushroom hunters who live in regions with cold winters, morels are synonymous with the spring" -Arora
