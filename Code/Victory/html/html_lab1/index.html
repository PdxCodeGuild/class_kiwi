Commodore Weston Bio

Eric Stanton Weston is a fictional character created by a Canadian author named Evan Currie. The now Commodore Weston was the leader of an elite fighter squadron known as the Archangel Squadron. His call sign was Raziel as was famously known by ground troops as the patriot saint of the western pacific for his success in leading air battles across the pacific. After the war, he received a commission for earth’s first interstellar vessel. In his first mission, his crew engaged in an interstellar battle between two advanced human races, siding with the inferior race. His actions led to an alliance that was critical in saving the earth from actions of the superior foe. However, the success was credited with Eric’s superior tactical skills. His squadron has successfully repelled three incursions from the foe and recently was able to capture a fleet admiral from an enemy invasion. At the moment, no one knows where his squadron is, it is safe though to believe that they are conducting patrols in deep space that will help keep the planet safe.
