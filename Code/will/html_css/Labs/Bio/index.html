Carlos Ray "Chuck" Norris (born March 10, 1940) is an American martial artist and actor. He is a black belt in Tang Soo Do, Brazilian jiu jitsu and judo. He is most well known for his role in Walker, Texas Ranger as well as starring in movies such as The Delta Force and The Expendables.

"When the boogie man goes to sleep, he checks his closet for me" - Chuck Norris


He has called the following locations over the years home:

  • Ryan, Oklahoma
  • Torrance, California
  • Osan Air Base
  • March Air Force Base
